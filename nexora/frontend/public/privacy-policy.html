Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

Nexora is an AI-powered email intelligence tool for college students. We take your privacy seriously. This policy explains what data we access, how we use it, and how we protect it.

1. Information We Access

When you connect your Google account, Nexora requests access to the following Google data:

  • Gmail (read-only): We read your email messages to categorize them (assignments, hackathons, placements, meetings, etc.) and extract deadlines and action items.
  • Google Calendar Events: We read your calendar events to surface meeting reminders and upcoming deadlines.
  • Basic Profile: Your name, email address, and profile picture for account creation.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Classify and organize your emails by category and priority using AI
  • Extract upcoming deadlines, assignments, and meeting details
  • Answer natural language questions about your inbox ("Nexora Brain")
  • Send you smart notifications for high-priority events

We do not sell, share, or transfer your email data to any third parties.

3. Data Storage & Security

  • Your Gmail OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest using AES-256 encryption before being stored in our database.
  • Email content is stored securely in our database and is only accessible by you.
  • All API communication happens over HTTPS/TLS.
  • We use read-only Gmail access — Nexora cannot send, delete, or modify any emails.

4. Data Retention

Email data is stored for as long as your account is active. You can delete your account and all associated data at any time by contacting us at the email below.

5. Third-Party Services

  • Google APIs: Used for Gmail and Calendar access. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Gemini API: Used for AI email classification and Q&A. Email content may be sent to Google's API for processing. Subject to Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Google API Services User Data Policy

Nexora's use and transfer of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

7. Your Rights

  • You can revoke Nexora's access to your Google account at any time via Google Account Permissions.
  • You can request deletion of all your data by contacting us.
